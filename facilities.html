Campus Facilities

A holistic environment equipped with modern infrastructure to ensure the best learning experience.

Hostel Accommodation

Separate, highly secure, and comfortable hostel facilities are available for both boys and girls. Equipped with modern amenities, study rooms, and hygienic mess facilities.

Transport Facility

Our dedicated fleet of buses ensures safe and timely transportation for students across various routes in the city, making daily commutes smooth and stress-free.

Practical Labs

State-of-the-art anatomy, physiology, and fundamental nursing laboratories equipped with advanced mannequins and medical instruments for precise hands-on learning.

Central Library

A well-stocked library housing an extensive collection of medical journals, reference books, and digital resources to support the academic and research needs of students.

Clinical Training

Robust tie-ups with leading multi-specialty government and private hospitals to provide students with rigorous real-world clinical exposure and patient care experience.

Smart Classrooms

Spacious, well-ventilated classrooms with digital projectors and smart boards designed to foster interactive and immersive theoretical learning sessions.
