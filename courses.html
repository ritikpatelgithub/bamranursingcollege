Academic Programs

Industry-focused nursing curricula designed for aspiring healthcare professionals.

Only for Girls

ANM

Auxiliary Nursing Midwifery

The ANM program is a comprehensive 2-year diploma designed to train individuals to become competent auxiliary nurse midwives. The curriculum focuses on preparing students to function as effective healthcare providers in rural and urban community settings, emphasizing maternal and child health.

Duration 2 Years Full-Time
Eligibility Criteria +2 Pass (Any Stream)
Target Gender Exclusively for Females
Placement 100% Job Assistance
Apply for ANM Course

GNM

General Nursing & Midwifery

The GNM course is an extensive 3-year diploma program dedicated to preparing dedicated students into fully functional, efficient nurses. It offers heavy emphasis on hospital methodologies, surgical procedures, intensive care, and advanced nursing protocols necessary for large hospitals and clinics.

Duration 3 Years Full-Time
Eligibility Criteria +2 Pass (Any Stream)
Target Gender For Boys & Girls
Placement 100% Job Assistance
