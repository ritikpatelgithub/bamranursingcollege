Admission Procedure

Eligibility Criteria

  • Successful completion of 10+2 (Any Stream) from a recognized board.
  • Must meet age criteria as per Indian Nursing Council rules.
  • Physically and mentally fit.

Step-by-Step Process

1

Submit Application

Fill out the quick online application form provided here with accurate details.

2

Counseling Session

Our admission counselor will contact you to discuss your career goals and course suitability.

3

Document Verification

Submit 10th/12th marksheets, ID proof, and photographs at the college admissions desk.

4

Enrollment & Seat Confirmation

Pay the admission fees to finalize your seat and collect your enrollment kit.
