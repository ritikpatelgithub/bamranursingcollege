About Bamra College

Delivering excellence in nursing education and healthcare training since our inception.

Who We Are

Dedicated to Shaping
Exceptional Nurses

Bamra Nursing College is a premier institution established with the core objective of imparting high-quality nursing education. We believe that nurses form the backbone of the healthcare system, and our thorough curriculum reflects our dedication to the highest medical standards.

Approved by the Health & Family Welfare Department and DMET, Government of Odisha, we provide a holistic learning environment. Our students are trained rigorously in theoretical knowledge and practical clinical procedures to prepare them for demanding roles in the real world.

Best Curriculum

Aligned closely with modern healthcare demands.

Real-world Training

Extensive practical sessions in leading hospitals.

Our Vision

To emerge as a globally recognized center of excellence in nursing education and research. We envision our institution as the birthplace of compassionate, highly-skilled medical professionals who transform patient care standards locally and internationally.

Our Mission

  • To provide value-based education driving clinical competence.
  • To bridge the gap between medical academics and practical hospital demands.
  • To foster ethical practice and empathy in all our graduates.
